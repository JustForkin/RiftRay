## HMD Rendering in brief - HMD is an I/O device - Gyro/magnet for orientation - Optional external cam for position(outside-in) - **Latency** is paramount (motion to photon) - No deep pipelines, minimal multi-pass rendering - FPS is _mandatory_ - DK1@60Hz, DK2@75Hz, CB@90Hz - More is better - Side-by-side stereo framebuffer - Vertex shader cost x2, pixel shader cost unaffected - Geometry shader or instancing [may also be used](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/19x9XDjUvkW_9gsfsMQzt3hZbRNziVsoCEHOn4AercAc/edit#slide=id.g5790d2a7c_046) - Geometry is important again - Image space tricks (i.e. normal maps) look flat - Tessellation could be a bigger win
